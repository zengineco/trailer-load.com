How it works

Pieces ride in on the belt. You stack them into a trailer wall that has to hold up — support every box, keep the load balanced, lock complete rows into shelves, load hazmat low, and seal the wall before dispatch.

Build the wall

Select freight, place it, rotate it. Floor-up, support underneath, no floating boxes — the physics won't let you cheat gravity.

Load it right

Lock shelves, keep the center of gravity centered, fragile on top, hazmat below the waist line, and stuff the dead air inside tires.

Get your grade

Every wall is scored on integrity, density, pace, and safety — and ranked, from Rookie to Yard Legend.
