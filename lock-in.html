PRE-SORT MEETING
1
Grab & place. Pull a box off the chute, click the wall to set it. [R] rotates.Pieces queue in the chute — rotate before you place to fit tighter.
2
Build off the floor. Every piece needs support under it — nothing floats.A freestanding stack 4+ high with no wall beside it topples back to the belt.
3
Lock your shelves. Fill a full row to lock it; a locked shelf braces the wall above.Locked shelves also cap a hazmat safely beneath them.
4
Stage the non-cons. Tires, SP & oversize wait in the staging tray at the bottom — click, then wall it.Non-conveyables are the irregular freight the belt can't carry.
5
Fragile & glass ride on top. Never bury them — a crush is a write-up.Load them near the top with only light pieces above.
6
Hazmat low & locked. Floor it, cap it under a shelf.A hazmat in ANY collapse ends the round — keep it stable.
7
Lay tires flat. On its edge, a tire rolls the second the door opens.Standing one up is a safety warning — lay it flat before the door opens.
8
Bags spill smalls. Double-click a bag to fill the gaps a box can't reach.Out of smalls? Throw a box to summon a bag in a pinch.
9
Pack tight, then seal. Density is the job — fill, seal at height, beat the clock.Cube fill, not just height — the manager calls the target.
