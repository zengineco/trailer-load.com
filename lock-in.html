1
You're loading a trailer. Select a piece from the belt below, then click the wall to place it. Press R to rotate.
2
Special-Handling freight (SP) — heavy or awkward items a belt can't carry, flagged with red SP badges (you'll hear NCs, ICs, irregs, non-cons — all special handling) — stage in slots on the floor. They must be placed on the wall or you'll trigger a safety hazard.
3
Fill the floor first. Build from the bottom up. Every piece needs support underneath — a piece floating in midair will fail the legality check.
4
When you complete a full horizontal row — every cell filled — it LOCKS IN. That's a shelf. That's your structural anchor. That's how real walls get built.
5
FRAGILE pieces must go on top. Never place heavy freight directly above a fragile piece — it crunches and costs you score.
6
At 85% wall height, the dispatch clock starts. Seal the wall before time runs out for a bonus. Hit 92% to complete the load.
7
Smalls bags on the floor — double-click or long-press to break them into 5 gap-fill pieces. Essential for tight voids near the top.
8
Watch your Balance (COG) indicator. Alternating heavy pieces left and right keeps the wall stable and earns a better grade.
PRE-SORT MEETING Hub Mode · Management Not Present
1
You're loading a trailer. Belt's down there. Click something. Place it on the wall. Hit R to spin it. You know what rotating means.
2
Those red SP badges? Special handling. NCs, ICs, irregs, non-cons — same animal. Heavy. Awkward. Stage them on the floor, get them on the wall. Leave them sitting and the whole shift goes sideways.
3
Floor first. Always floor first. Every 22-year veteran who says otherwise has been off the docks for 10 years and counting.
4
Fill a complete row and it LOCKS IN. That's a shelf. That's a LOCKED IN. That's the one thing they never teach new hires and wonder why walls fall apart.
5
FRAGILE goes on top. Not on the bottom. Not in the middle. On. Top. You've done this before? Act like it.
6
At 85% the clock starts. The trailer doesn't wait. Neither does dispatch. Neither does the flight. Seal it.
7
Those smalls bags? Double-click them. Five gap-fillers come out. Use them. Don't leave holes you could've filled.
8
The COG bar. Left side heavy? Put something on the right. This isn't physics class. It's common sense. Go.
